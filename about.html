About Topex

TOPEX is one of the leading institutions in the meetings industry and event management. Located in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, We achieved considerable success at the level in the GCC and take the leadership in the world of organizing and carrying out exhibitions, conferences. We reached a wide range of success partners, since we supply giant events with all needed supplies, Also our flexibility and multi-tasking strategy helped us to do so. It enabled us to largely keep up with the requirements and managing the diverse factors in this sector, which gave us the excellence, Precession and leadership in the field.
